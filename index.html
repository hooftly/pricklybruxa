About Me

Hi! My name is Michelle Silva and I am a Certified Medical Esthetician spcializing in electrolysis, laser hair removal, piedicures and more. I also offer an array of spiritual services including Reiki and Tarot readings. I am dedicated to providing professional services for my clients at affordable rates. Aesthetics is my passion. I love my work and my clients and take pride in every moment spent with them.

I graduated the Full Medical Aesthetics program at the Alberta Academy of Aesthetics. Services include Manicures, Pedicures, Waxing, Lash Tint/Lift/Curl, Brow Tint, Dermaplaning, Electrolysis, Crystal Microdermabrasion, Facials, Chemical Peel, IPL Laser, Skin Rejuvenation and Relaxation Massage. Certified in Airbrush Tanning, Teeth Whitening and Henna Brow from the European Institute.
